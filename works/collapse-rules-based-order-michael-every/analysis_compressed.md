# Core Ideas: Compressed

## Main Thesis
- The collapse of the liberal world order ≠ American decline
- America built the order and is now choosing to dismantle it
- This is "prioritization, not abandonment"

## Economic Statecraft Framework
- Shift from economic policy (siloed) to economic statecraft (unified)
- All tools combined "like fingers and a thumb into a fist"
- The fundamental question: "What is GDP for?"
- Once answered, all other policy decisions follow logically

## Reverse Perestroika
- Trump attempting opposite of Gorbachev's failed reforms
- Gorbachev: command economy → market economy (collapsed)
- Trump: consumer economy → command/military economy (attempting)
- Risk: could fail just as catastrophically

## Geopolitical Strategy
- Monroe Doctrine explicitly back in effect
- Americas = American sphere (non-negotiable)
- Europe forced to self-defend ("wet paper bag" currently)
- Asia must be contested, not ceded to China
- Prevent Eurasian bloc formation at all costs

## Key Geographic Moves
- Venezuela: deny oil to China, not take it for US ("physical censorship")

- Latin America: parallel reindustrialization, "inclusive imperialism"

## The Fed as Battleground
- Control of Fed essential for transformation

## Defense Spending

## Stablecoin Strategy (The Stealth Weapon)
